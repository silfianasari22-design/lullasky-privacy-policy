Privacy Policy for LullaSky

Last updated: February 2024

1. Introduction

LullaSky ("we," "our," or "us") provides relaxation music for babies. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience for our users.

2. Information Collection

We do not collect any personal identification information (PII) from our users. Our application does not require you to create an account or provide your name, email, or phone number to function.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: For core app functionality.
  • AdMob (Google): To display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers to serve personalized ads.
  • Firebase Realtime Database: To provide and update online music playlists.

4. Children's Privacy

LullaSky is intended for parents to use for their children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent and aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: support@lullasky.com


Data Deletion Request

If you wish to request the deletion of any data collected by the LullaSky app (such as device identifiers or analytics data), you can contact us via email at: support@lullasky.com.

Upon receiving your request, we will process it within 7 business days. Please note that some data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention).
